+ What is ptext?

- ptext is a simple text based presenter.
- ptext is written in python

+ What is the inspiration behind ptext?

- I was looking for a super simple way of doing a text-based presentation

+ How to use it?

- It's really easy...
- $ ./ptext.py ppt.txt
  OR
  $ pytohn ppt.txt
- Hint
    'n' to go to the next slide
    'b' to go to the previous slide
    'q' to quit
